Fraud & Risk Analytics Dashboard

Role: BI Manager • Duration: 6 months • Stack: Azure, Snowflake, Power BI, Python

Context

High-volume online transactions required proactive risk monitoring and executive visibility.

Problem

No single source of truth for chargebacks, verification funnels, or anomaly signals.

Approach

  • Unified payment, account, and support data; defined KRIs and governance.
  • Built anomaly detection and outlier surfacing with scheduled Python jobs.
  • Delivered exec and investigator views in Power BI with row-level security.

Impact

  • Reduced chargeback exposure (trend reversal within 2 quarters).
  • Cut time-to-investigate by ~30% via consolidated views and alerts.
  • Improved audit readiness with lineage, ownership, and documentation.
