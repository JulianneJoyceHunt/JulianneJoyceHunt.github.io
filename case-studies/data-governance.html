Data Governance at Scale

Role: BI Manager • Duration: 4 months • Stack: Microsoft Purview, Snowflake, Power BI

Context

Rapid growth required clear ownership, lineage, and documentation.

Problem

Teams lacked a single catalog and governance process; audits were slow.

Approach

  • Implemented catalog + lineage in Purview; defined roles & stewardship.
  • Published governance playbook; embedded quality checks into pipelines.
  • Established change control and communication cadences.

Impact

  • Audit prep weeks → days; improved adoption of self-service analytics.
  • Higher data trust; fewer duplicate metrics.
