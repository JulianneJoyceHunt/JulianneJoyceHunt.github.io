Healthcare KPI Standardization

Role: Senior Business Analyst • Duration: 9 months • Stack: SQL Server, Looker, Tableau

Context

Multiple teams tracked overlapping metrics with conflicting definitions.

Problem

Leaders lacked consistent views for operational and clinical performance.

Approach

  • Aligned stakeholders on canonical KPI definitions and ownership.
  • Centralized 23+ data sources; modeled for consistency and reuse.
  • Rolled out KPI dashboards and data dictionary for self-service.

Impact

  • Faster decision cycles and reduced escalations.
  • Higher trust in numbers; improved compliance posture.
